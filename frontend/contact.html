Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account? ‚ñº

Click on "Join Community" button in the navigation. Fill in your name, email, phone, business category, and create a secure password. You'll be able to access your dashboard immediately after registration.

I forgot my password. What do I do? ‚ñº

On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" link. Enter your registered email address and you'll receive password reset instructions within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it in your inbox.

Can I change my business category later? ‚ñº

Yes! You can update your business category anytime from your profile settings. Go to Dashboard ‚Üí Profile Settings ‚Üí Edit Category. Your products will be organized correctly once you make the change.

How do I add products to my store? ‚ñº

Log in to your dashboard and click "+ Add Product". Fill in product details including title, description, price, category, quantity, and upload high-quality images. Click "Add Product" and your product will be live immediately.

What image formats are supported? ‚ñº

We support JPG, PNG, GIF, and WebP formats. Maximum file size is 5MB per image, and you can upload up to 5 images per product. For best quality, use high-resolution images (at least 1200x800 pixels).

How much does it cost to sell on EmpowerHer? ‚ñº

Registration and account creation are completely FREE! We don't charge any listing fees or subscription costs. We only take a small commission (5-10%) when you make a sale. This helps us maintain the platform and provide support.

What payment methods do you accept? ‚ñº

We accept all major credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, digital wallets (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and NEFT transfers. Your payments are secured with bank-level encryption.

How do I get paid for my sales? ‚ñº

Payments are deposited directly to your bank account. You can set your bank details in the Payment Settings section of your dashboard. Payouts are processed every week, and you'll receive the funds within 2-3 business days.

How long does it take to process refunds? ‚ñº

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the return is approved. The refund will be credited back to the customer's original payment method. Our team will keep you updated on the status throughout the process.

Are there free guides and resources available? ‚ñº

Absolutely! Visit our Resources page for free business guides, pricing templates, marketing strategies, branding tips, and access to recommended tools. All resources are completely FREE and designed to help you grow your business.

Can I attend live training sessions? ‚ñº

Yes! We host weekly live training sessions on topics like marketing, pricing, customer service, and scaling your business. Sessions are free for all registered members. Check your email for upcoming session dates and registration links.

How can I connect with other sellers? ‚ñº

Join our community forums, WhatsApp groups, and category-specific communities. You can collaborate, share tips, ask questions, and build relationships with fellow women entrepreneurs. It's a supportive network built for mutual growth!

The website is slow. What can I do? ‚ñº

Try clearing your browser cache, disabling extensions, or using a different browser. Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If issues persist, contact support@empowerher.com with details about the problem.

I'm having trouble uploading images. Help! ‚ñº

Check that image files are less than 5MB and in supported formats (JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP). Ensure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari). If problems continue, try compressing the image or contact our support team for assistance.

How do I delete my account? ‚ñº

You can request account deletion from your Profile Settings ‚Üí Account Settings ‚Üí Delete Account. We'll keep your data for 30 days in case you change your mind. After that, all your information will be permanently deleted.

Didn't find your answer?

Our support team is ready to help! Reach out using the contact form above or send us an email.
